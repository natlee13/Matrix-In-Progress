About Matrix

Matrix Property Services was formed in 1992 by brothers Barry and Glen Osbourne. Both already had years of experience working in the trades and decided it was time to start their own business.

Covering the wider Auckland region, Matrix offers a comprehensive service to suit all of your domestic and commercial requirements. We are proud of our approach to work and strive to maintain the highest standards at all times.

Our prompt and reliable service makes us your number one choice for property maintenance.

If your home or office is looking tired and needs sprucing up, then look to the experts for advice and contact us for free estimate no obligation quote...
